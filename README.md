# TimeBlind

**TimeBlind** is a minimalist, web-based ritual tool designed to help you reclaim your time sovereignty.

In a world of constant notification, TimeBlind provides a 120-second "sanctuary" for your mind. No distractions, no tracking—just you and your intent.

## The Rituals
TimeBlind offers three distinct modes to reset your cognitive state:

* **Silence**: For clearing the mind and deep breathing.
* **Knowledge**: A flow of scientific facts to shift your focus.
* **Reflection**: A structured 4-step guided introspection to align your actions.

## How it works
1.  **Define**: Input your current focus or intent.
2.  **Ritualize**: Choose your mode and breathe.
3.  **Commit**: Take the action you reflected upon.

## Philosophy
TimeBlind is not a productivity tool. It is an infrastructure for intention. It is built to be:
* **Lightweight**: Zero external dependencies.
* **Private**: All data stays in your browser (LocalStorage).
* **Sovereign**: You own your focus.
